Our Story

Currently, World Billiards has effectively introduced its brand to the global billiards community. Internationally, we have partnered with renowned brands such as Aramith, Iwan Simonis, Hollywood, Uylin, Klemach, Andy, Bilking, IBS, etc. to distribute authentic products to consumers. We are continually reaching out to more brands that Vietnamese consumers adore so that we can bring in more products and assist customers in purchasing at better prices. We are proud to be the industry leader in the use of cutting-edge machinery in the production of pool tables. Every component of the table has been meticulously selected and imported directly from overseas suppliers. This is why our pool tables are unique, long-lasting, and do not degrade over time like other pool tables on the market.
